SHOP

HealthyMe plans on forming a shop feature for you to buy Pepper the pig new outfits and characters. In order to create an incentive for users to complete their tracx everyday and stay motivated, this plan awards them coinz that can be stored in their bank. With these coinz, users can buy new outfits for Pepper like AstroPepper and Doctor Pepper. They can also unlock two other characters, Ron and Lily, who also each have their own unique outfits and accessories. We hope that this future category will improve user experience and satisfaction along with aligning to our mission.

TRACX

Our Tracx program is starting soon! It will consist of three tracks as of now: meals, physical exercise and daily tasks. Each track has different features but all focus on tracking and measuring data to achieve your planned goals. Goal-keeping is very important to a healthy lifestyle and with our tracx program, it is easy to achieve your goals. Our meals tracx will track whether you have had your meals everyday, helping with those who struggle with a stable eating schedule. Our workout tracx will track how long you have completed an exercise/workout along with the amount of times you have worked out in a week according to your set standard. Finally, our tasks tracx will allow you to keep track of how long you have been doing a task along with whether the task is not completed, in progress or completed.
