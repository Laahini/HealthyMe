Welcome to TRACX

Tracx is a program created by HealthyMe to help you keep track of your meals, workouts and goals along with other daily tasks you might have.
We know how difficult it is to maintain a certain weight or measure your goals and actually achieving them. Who knows how many things we say we will do but never actually fulfill! This time, we'll help you mean what you say. Tracx will keep track of the time that you have worked out for, what meals you ate, and what tasks you completed. It's simple to use and easy to organize!
As of now, Tracx has three main tracks for our program: meals, physical exercise, and tasks. Check out each section below!

MEAL TRACX

The Meal Tracs program is very simple to use and helps you keep track of whether you have eaten your meals or not. Eating disorders, stress and busy work days can all make you have irregular eating habits. HealhyMe's Meal Tracx program keeps track of when you have had your meals and aims to support your goal of having a healthier eating schedule.

Here are the steps:

  • Wake up in the morning and eat breakfast
  • Click the breakfast button to mark off as you ate breakfast
  • Repeat the same process for lunch and dinner after you have ate them
  • Click the water button if you have drank at least 8 cups of water
  • Click Finished for the Day and repeat tomorrow!
